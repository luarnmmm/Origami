Origami Balloon

Step 1

Start with a square sheet of paper

Step 2

Fold the paper into a triangle by joining the bottom-right corner to the upper-left corner

Step 3

Unfold the paper

Step 4

Repeat steps 2-3Â this time joining the bottom-left corner to the upper-right corner



Step 5

Fold the paper horizontally (hamburger style) into a rectangle

Step 6

Unfold the paper

Step 7

Fold the paper from the center of the right edge down to the center of the bottom edge making sure you connect both centers together which will make a right triangle shape

Step 8

While pressing down on the previous fold, refold the upper right corner along the diagonal line and then connect the upper right triangle to the bottom right triangle

Step 9

Repeat steps 7-8 using the left side of the paper



Step 10

Once done you should have one big triangle

Step 11

Fold the bottom right corner to the center of the triangle making sure you are only folding the top triangle

Step 12

Repeat step 11 using the left side of the triangle

Step 13

Flip the paper over

Step 14

Repeat steps 11-12



Step 15

Fold the top right layer of the diamond to the center

Step 16

Repeat step 16 on the left side of the diamond

Step 17

Flip the paper over

Step 18

Repeat steps 15-16



Step 19

Grab the top left flap and insert it into the left triangle pocket



Step 20

Repeat step 19 on the right side



Step 21

Flip the paper over

Step 22

Repeat steps 19-20







Step 23

Turn the paper sideways (90 degrees to the right) and fold the top and bottom edges of the paper horizontally on each side

Step 24

Hold the balloon with the hole on top and grab each corner

Step 25

Blow into the hole to inflate the balloon

Done!

You made your own origami balloon!
