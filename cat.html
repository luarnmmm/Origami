Origami Cat

Step 1

Start with a square piece of origami paper.

Step 2

Fold the paper in half by taking the left corner and folding it to the right corner.

Step 3

Unfold it, and then fold the upper left part and right sides to the center part.

Steps 4

Fold the bottom left part and the right sides to the center part.

Steps 5

Fold the paper in half by taking the bottom corner part and folding it to the top.

Step 6

Fold Â¾ most of the flap part to the bottom.

Steps 7

Open the pocket space on the left side and push down and fold the left side to a triangle. Then, open the pocket space on the right side and push down and fold the right side to a triangle.

Steps 8

Fold the piece as is in half by folding the right side over to the left side.

Steps 9

Fold the very thin triangle to the right side which will make the tail.

Steps 10

After, unfold the tail and open it and bend the tail along its creases and then close the figure.

Step 12

Fold the bottom of the corner on both sides of the figure near the tails on both sides.

Steps 13

After, open up the body and you can see the cat.

Done!

Put your index fingers and thumbs under each corner.
